Heath Matthews, the go-to sports science and medicine practitioner for top sports persons in India, has spent the majority of his career pushing the boundaries of his profession to deliver outstanding results.

He has worked with professional football, rugby and cycling teams in South Africa, and professional tennis circuits where he toured with multiple world No.1’s and Grand Slam Champions like Mahesh Bhupathi, Leander Paes, Wesley Moodie, Kai Kanepi and Sania Mirza.

Heath founded Mumbai’s first fully integrated Sports Science and Medicine Centre at Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital and was the Head of Sports Science and Medicine at Sir H.N Reliance Foundation Hospital. He has been working with the TATA Group and the Indian Olympic Gold Quest for their top 10 Olympic athletes preparing for the recently concluded Tokyo Olympics.

Heath brings his learnings from decades of elite athlete management to create a revolutionary platform that will push the boundaries of human performance.
